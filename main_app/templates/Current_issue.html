COVID-19 Impact on Narmada River Water Quality

This study examines the significant changes in water quality parameters of the Narmada River during the COVID-19 lockdown period, comparing pre-pandemic and post-pandemic data to assess environmental recovery.

First Record of Platypria erinaceus in Rajasthan

Documenting the first-ever sighting of Platypria erinaceus in the Thar Desert region, this research provides insights into the species' adaptation mechanisms and potential ecological implications for the region.

Published: 20 July 2025 | Category: Zoology

Genetic Stability of In Vitro G. glabra via RAPD

Investigating the genetic fidelity of in vitro propagated Glycyrrhiza glabra using RAPD markers, this study contributes to conservation efforts and sustainable cultivation of this medicinally important plant species.

Published: 20 July 2025 | Category: Biotechnology
